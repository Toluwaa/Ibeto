KINGS PALACE HOTEL. LTD






Established in March 1985, Kings Palace Hotel has become synonymous with hospitality. Located at the heart of Nnewi, the hotel has played frontline role in theeconomic and tourism development in Anambra State in particular and Nigeria ingeneral. It has 44 rooms and 6 suits fully air-conditioned and tastefully furnished with TV and Satellite channels, complementary mini bar, restaurant, laundry andConference Hall. Kings Palace Hotel has remained a Tourist’s delight. In additionto providing 24 hour room service, it also offers buffet and a’la carte and out-doorcatering services.
